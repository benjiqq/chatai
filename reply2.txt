1. Take Short Breaks: Start with taking a short break to rejuvenate. A quick walk or some stretching exercises can help to recharge your energy.

2. Prioritize Tasks: Identify the most important tasks and focus on those. It's not about getting everything done, but rather the most crucial ones.

3. Stay Hydrated and Eat Healthy: Drink plenty of water and eat light, nutritious meals. Heavy meals can make you feel sluggish.

4. Listen to Music: Listening to some soft, instrumental music can help to improve concentration and increase productivity.

5. Take a Power Nap: If possible, a short nap of 20-30 minutes can help to improve mood, alertness and performance.

6. Stay Organized: Keep your workspace clean and organized. A cluttered desk can lead to a cluttered mind.

7. Practice Mindfulness: Try some mindfulness techniques like deep breathing or meditation. It can help to reduce stress and increase focus.

8. Limit Distractions: Turn off any unnecessary notifications on your phone or computer.

Remember, it's okay to have off days. Don't push yourself too hard and make sure to take care of your health first.
